The world's most popular youtube blogger PewDiePie has become a streamer on Youtube Gaming

Felix 'PewDiePie' Chelberg-signed an exclusive contract to conduct streams on the Youtube Gaming platform. In principle, it seems that the news was voiced by "Captain obvious". The most popular blogger from youtube started streaming on youtube gaming. On this note, you can already move on to another article. However, I want to point out other important points that "flow" from this news. Bloggers become a streamer? The fact is that streaming as an activity is more interactive in itself, and over time (with the growing quality of the Internet), streamers will displace bloggers because they are more interactive. They are closer to the public, they are broadcasting right now. I will never forget how during the broadcast of "Dota 2 Major" a guy and a girl sat at the next table in a coffee shop (they clearly had a date) and watched the broadcast. Can you imagine him watching a video recorded a couple of days ago? No, there's no need, and it makes sense to watch the live stream right now, whether you're on a date or somewhere else.
